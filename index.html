The Foundation

Built on Belief.

"Every family deserves a plot of earth they can truly call their own. Gurmukh Park is our promise to make that possible in Kumhari."
— Jai Baba Builders

Kumhari sits at a rare intersection — close enough to Raipur and Durg to offer every city convenience, yet far enough to offer something increasingly rare: peace. When Jai Baba Builders looked for the right location for our first project, Kumhari chose us as much as we chose it.

Gurmukh Park is not just a plotted development. It is a carefully planned community — 8 acres of land thoughtfully laid out with wide internal roads, green spaces, and proximity to the sacred surroundings of Kaivalya Dham. We wanted the neighbourhood itself to feel like a blessing.

As first-time developers, we bring something established builders often lose — the hunger to prove ourselves on every square foot. Your trust is our first project. We will not waste it.
